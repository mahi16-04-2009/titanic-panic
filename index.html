The Titanic mystery

What Happened?

The RMS Titanic sank in the early hours of April 15, 1912, after hitting an iceberg. It was called “unsinkable” — yet over 1,500 lives were lost. Why was such a massive ship allowed to sail with such few lifeboats?

Timeline

April 10, 1912 – Titanic departs from Southampton.

April 14, 11:40 PM – Hits iceberg.

April 15, 2:20 AM – Ship sinks.

The Unanswered Questions

Did a fire in the coal bunker weaken the hull? Why were warnings about icebergs ignored? Was it swapped with its sister ship, Olympic, in an insurance scam?
